One Year, One Move, One Love

This past year has been one of the most beautiful—and one of the most challenging—I’ve ever known.
Loving you felt so right, so effortless, like my heart had finally found its home.
But I won’t pretend it was all easy. Emotions ran deep, and some days were heavier than others.
Still, through it all, there’s one thing I’ve never doubted: you and me.
I know harder days may still come, but isn’t that what we always said? P and Chi Chi against the world.
If this is what you want too, I’m here—completely.
Ready to fight for us.
Because a love like this… doesn’t come twice.

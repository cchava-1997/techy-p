One Year, One Move, One Love

This year turned out nothing like I planned—
all because of one night that changed everything I thought my future would be.
Loving you has been the easiest thing my heart has ever done,
and somehow, the most challenging too.
It was all easy.
Emotions ran deep, and some days were heavier than others.
Still, through it all—and with the harder days still ahead—
isn’t that what this is about?
P and Chi Chi against the world.
So today, I made something for us—
something simple, filled with little moments only we’d remember.
Something that that will keep us going on those hard days.
